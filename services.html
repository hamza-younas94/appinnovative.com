The Process

01

PROJECT KICKOFF
AND TECHNICAL ANALYSIS

AppInnovative collect all the required information that is needed to prepare the technical scope. Project estimations are created and after client's approval, handed over to the Project Manager.
02

IDEA EVALUATIONS
AND BRAND IDENTITY CREATION

Wireframes, color theme and prototypes are build in this stage. Client has full say in the entire design process. After the designs are approved and prototypes are made, it moves to development stage.
03

DESIGN AND
DEVELOPMENT

All the designs are hand coded to covert it into UI along with the required animations and transitions. Head-down development is executed and the product is prepared for a beta launch.
04

QUALITY ASSURANCE
AND POST LAUNCH ACTIVITES

At AppInnovative, we test the product inside-out before it hits the market. We process the code through all phase of testings and post-launch activities are prepared for the final publishing.
