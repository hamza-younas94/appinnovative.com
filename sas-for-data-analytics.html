How do I take the course?

You will be given one-to-one session online using webinar applications. Instructor will share his working screen and you can follow along. One can have session one-to-one or in a group of more than 1 to maximum 5 in single session.

The best part is that you learn on your own schedule mutually agreed with the instructor. Since it's a paid course, we will have to give you timeline and little course work to get you ready for a life out there. You will have 60 days to schedule your designated hours with the instructor.
