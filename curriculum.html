About the course

Web design is fun and exciting, and we love it but it becomes intimidating when we start looking at all the codes and difficult syntaxes. Not any more!

Introducing Webflow
Webflow is an amazing next generation tool that bridge a huge gap between the designers and the frontend developer. It allows us to design visually all the web pages and it converts the entire design into frontend html/css/js ready for production. Besides this, webflow also enable us to complete the production and host the website with webflow itself.

In this course, we will take you step by step form the basics of design to a final published website. You will learn how to convert your static designs into a fully responsive functional website.
